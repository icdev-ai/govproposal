# Standard DoD Proposal Outline

## Volume I: Technical Approach

1. Executive Summary
2. Technical Understanding
   - 2.1 Understanding of the Requirement
   - 2.2 Current Environment Assessment
   - 2.3 Technical Challenges and Risks
3. Technical Solution
   - 3.1 Solution Architecture
   - 3.2 Tools and Technologies
   - 3.3 Integration Approach
   - 3.4 Security Architecture
4. Technical Management
   - 4.1 Systems Engineering Approach
   - 4.2 Configuration Management
   - 4.3 Quality Assurance
5. Transition Plan
   - 5.1 Phase-In Approach
   - 5.2 Knowledge Transfer
   - 5.3 Risk Mitigation

## Volume II: Management Approach

1. Management Summary
2. Organizational Structure
   - 2.1 Corporate Overview
   - 2.2 Program Organization Chart
   - 2.3 Key Personnel
3. Staffing Plan
   - 3.1 Labor Category Descriptions
   - 3.2 Staffing Timeline
   - 3.3 Recruitment and Retention
4. Program Management
   - 4.1 Task Order Management
   - 4.2 Risk Management
   - 4.3 Communication Plan
5. Subcontractor/Teaming Management
6. Security Management
   - 6.1 Facility Clearance
   - 6.2 Personnel Security
   - 6.3 Information Security (NIST 800-171)

## Volume III: Past Performance

1. Past Performance Summary Matrix
2. Relevant Contract References (3-5)
   - Contract Number, Agency, Period of Performance
   - Relevance to Current Requirement
   - Technical Approach Used
   - Results and Outcomes (quantified)
   - Customer Contact Information
3. CPARS Summary (if available)

## Volume IV: Cost/Price

1. Cost Summary
2. Basis of Estimate (BOE)
3. Labor Rate Justification
4. Subcontractor Pricing
5. Other Direct Costs (ODCs)
6. Travel Estimates
